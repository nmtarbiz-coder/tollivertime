About Nathan

I Bought My First Rolex
and Never Looked Back.

In March of 2025, I put a Rolex on my wrist for the first time. Something clicked. I’d never worn jewelry of any kind — not a ring, not a chain, nothing. But there was something about that watch that stopped me cold. The weight of it. The craftsmanship. The way it made me feel.

That was the moment Tolliver Timepieces was born — I just didn’t know it yet.

“I’ve always been a tinkerer. Boats, trucks, cars, 4-wheelers, side-by-sides, houses, properties — I buy them, fix them, use them, and sell them. Watches are just the latest chapter.”
The Story

How a Lifelong Habit Became a Business

From One Watch to Many

After that first Rolex, I did what any curious person does — I started trying other brands. Tudor. Omega. IWC. Panerai. I wanted to understand the differences, feel them on the wrist, learn what made each one special.

Before long, I had more watches than wrists. So I started selling a few. Connected with other dealers online. Started studying the market seriously — pricing, authentication, condition grading, platforms. I was hooked.

A Different Kind of Dealer

I’m not a chain store or a pawn shop. I’m one person who genuinely loves this hobby. When you buy from me, you’re talking directly to the guy who sourced the watch, inspected it, researched it, and priced it fairly.

There’s a whole world of people who love watches — and I love being part of that community. Every transaction is a conversation, and I enjoy every one of them.
