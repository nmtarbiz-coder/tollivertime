Why Buy a Pre-Owned Rolex?

A pre-owned Rolex Submariner sells for $8,000–$12,000 vs $10,950+ retail — when you can even find one at an authorized dealer.

Rolex watches have historically held or appreciated in value. A pre-owned Rolex is not just a purchase — it's an asset.

Models We Carry

Rolex Collections

Rolex Submariner

The icon. Date and no-date versions, steel and two-tone. One of the most traded luxury watches in the world.

Rolex Datejust

Classic dress watch in 36mm and 41mm. Fluted, smooth, and jubilee variants. Endless dial options.

Rolex GMT-Master II

The traveler's watch. Batman, Pepsi, and Root Beer references. Highly sought after.

Rolex Daytona

The chronograph of champions. Steel Daytona remains one of the most coveted watches on the market.

Rolex Oyster Perpetual

Pure, clean, no-frills Rolex. Available in multiple sizes and dial colors. Excellent value.

Rolex Explorer

Built for adventure. Explorer I and II — the understated choice for Rolex connoisseurs.

Rolex Sky-Dweller

Rolex's most complicated sports watch. Annual calendar and dual timezone in one.

Rolex Sea-Dweller

Deep water capability with helium escape valve. The professional diver's Rolex.
